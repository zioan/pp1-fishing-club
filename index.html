Together since 1960

Apen Fishing Club in a nutshell

Our beloved club was first established in May 1960 with 3 original members. In the first three months, 152 happy anglers joined the club, and we continued to grow exponentially since then.

Today we are proud to announce that our club has over 5000 members and the list continues to grow. The Apen Fishing Club is like one big family where everyone shares their fishing stories and tips. Whether you're new to fishing or a pro, you'll find friends here who share your passion.

It is all about making memories and enjoying the outdoors with friends who love fishing as much as you do.

You can follow this Wikipedia link if you would like to learn more about the founders of our club.

How to become a member

Young or old, it doesn't matter!

Adults

Rules:

  • cannot use more than 4 fishing rods at the same time;

Required documents:

  • valid fishing license;
  • valid ID;

Fees:

  • the admission fee is €40;
  • the annual fee is €60;

After 12 years old

Rules:

  • must always be accompanied by an adult who has a valid fishing license. The adult must be a member of our club;
  • cannot use more than 1 fishing rod at the same time;

Required documents:

  • the adult who is going to accompany the child to the fishing parties must present a valid ID and fishing license, as well as proof of membership in our club;
  • a legal form of identification for the child and a proof of the relationship between him and the accompanying adult;

Fees:

  • there is no admission fee;
  • the annual fee is €10;

After 14 years old

Rules:

  • cannot use more than 2 fishing rods at the same time;
  • must be accompanied by an adult who has a valid fishing license. The adult must be a member of our club;
  • after the age of 16, the young angler can participate unaccompanied in fishing parties if he have understood the club regulations. The adult is responsible for his actions;

Required documents:

  • valid fishing license;
  • the adult who is going to accompany the child to the fishing parties must present a valid ID and fishing license, as well as proof of membership in our club;
  • a legal form of identification for the child and a proof of the relationship between him and the accompanying adult;

Fees:

  • the admission fee is €20;
  • the annual fee is €30;

Guest ticket

Guest tickets can be purchased from our office or any nearby gas station.

Rules:

  • at least 18 years old;
  • each ticket owner can use maximum of 4 fishing rods at the same time;

Required documents:

  • valid ID;
  • valid fishing license;

Fees:

  • 1 day €6;
  • 3 days €12;
  • 7 days €20;
  • 30 days €60;

* Admission fee and first annual fee must be paid upon registration.
